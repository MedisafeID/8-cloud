How We Work

Engagement Models

Whichever service area you need, you can bring us in three ways. Most clients start with an assessment and scale up from there.

Project-Based

Fixed-scope delivery

A defined outcome: a migration, an application, an audit, scoped, priced, and delivered against a clear timeline. Best when you know what you need built.

Staff Augmentation

Embedded engineers

Senior cloud, AI, or security engineers who plug directly into your existing team and sprint cadence. Best when you have the roadmap and need the hands.

Managed Partnership

Ongoing technology partner

An always-on relationship covering architecture decisions, incident response, and continuous modernization. Best when technology is core to how you compete.

Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

A few things most prospective clients ask before the first connect.

Do we need to know exactly what we want before contacting 8Cloud?+
No. Most engagements start with a short discovery phase where we assess what's running today and map out realistic options together. You don't need a finished spec, just a real problem worth solving.
Can you work alongside our existing in-house team?+
Yes, this is one of our most common engagement shapes. We can embed engineers directly into your existing sprints, hand off a completed project to your team, or run independently with regular syncs. We'll match the model to how your team already works, not the other way around.
What size organizations do you typically work with?+
We work across the range, from venture-backed startups migrating off legacy hosting to larger organizations modernizing core systems. Engagement size scales with the scope of the problem, not the size of the company.
How quickly can a project start?+
Most engagements begin within one to two weeks of a signed scope, once we've completed a discovery call and aligned on priorities. Urgent security incidents or active outages can move significantly faster.
