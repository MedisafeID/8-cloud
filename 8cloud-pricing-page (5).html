Common questions
What's the difference between monthly and lifetime?
+
Monthly gives you full access for $49/mo (Standard) or $79/mo (Pro) — cancel anytime. Lifetime is a one-time payment of $249 for Standard or $399 for Pro, and you keep access to everything forever, including all future certifications we add.
What does the Career Launch package include?
+
Everything in the Pro plan plus a dedicated career marketer who handles your resume, LinkedIn, job applications, mock interviews, salary negotiation, personal branding, and direct referrals to hiring managers. Support lasts 6 months or until you're hired.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
+
Yes — all plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us and we'll refund you in full.
How long does it take to get certified?
+
It depends on the certification. Most associate-level certs take 20-35 hours of study. Professional and specialist certs typically take 40-55 hours. You can go at your own pace.
Can I upgrade from Standard to Career Launch later?
+
Yes — you can upgrade at any time. If you're on the monthly plan we'll credit what you've already paid toward the Career Launch package.
